Here is the list of 2NF database tables with column names for your project.

### 1. User
This table stores information about the staff who use the system.
* `UserID` (Primary Key)
* `FirstName`
* `LastName`
* `UserName`
* `PasswordHash`
* `Role`

***

### 2. Customer
This table stores basic details about customers for sales records.
* `CustomerID` (Primary Key)
* `FirstName`
* `LastName`
* `PhoneNumber`
* `Email`

***

### 3. Supplier
This table holds information about the vendors.
* `SupplierID` (Primary Key)
* `SupplierName`
* `ContactName`
* `ContactEmail`
* `ContactPhone`

***

### 4. Product
This table represents your inventory.
* `ProductID` (Primary Key)
* `ProductName`
* `Category`
* `Description`
* `Price`
* `Cost`
* `StockQuantity`
* `SKU`
* `SupplierID` (Foreign Key)

***

### 5. Sale
This table records overall transaction details.
* `SaleID` (Primary Key)
* `SaleDate`
* `TotalAmount`
* `PaymentMethod`
* `SalesRepresentativeID` (Foreign Key)
* `CustomerID` (Foreign Key, Nullable)

***

### 6. SaleItem
This table details the specific products in a sale.
* `SaleItemID` (Primary Key)
* `SaleID` (Foreign Key)
* `ProductID` (Foreign Key)
* `Quantity`
* `UnitPrice`
* `DiscountApplied`

***

### 7. Expense
This table is for tracking business expenses.
* `ExpenseID` (Primary Key)
* `ExpenseDate`
* `ExpenseCategory`
* `Description`
* `Amount`
* `UserID` (Foreign Key, Nullable)

***

### 8. Discount
This table stores information about price offers.
* `DiscountID` (Primary Key)
* `DiscountName`
* `DiscountType`
* `DiscountValue`
* `ApplicableTo`
* `StartDate`
* `EndDate`
